ITEM F - App Development

Status: Completed - 25/03/2016

This term in year 12 Information Technology Systems class has been approached by an educational institute ‘Smithfield Early Learning Centre’. Due to recent studies, a trend has been found which affects the way children learn and play due to the impact of smartphones and tablets as statistics also show the continued rise of smartphone and tablet usage especially by children and the increased rate of apps developed for such devices. Following the trend ‘Smithfield Early Learning Centre’ has requested an application to be developed that can be employed on to smartphones and tablets, providing a digital educational platform acting as an educational tool for children around the age of 2 – 5.
